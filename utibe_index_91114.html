Created and run by Independent Software, The Whiteboard is an online community building platform for Connecticut startup ecosystem. By sharing exciting stories of local entrepreneurs, covering news and important events, and providing issue-focused articles, The Whiteboard supports the wins of Connecticut startups and advances entrepreneurship in our State.

The Startup Roadshow is 9-month long initiative of The Whiteboard to celebrate and explore Connecticut's diverse startup communities. Virtually, through The Whiteboard, we bring our readers into a different region of Connecticut each month, profiling top and up-and-coming entrepreneurs and revealing the uniqueness of the place. At the end of each month, we host an event that supports the local entrepreneurs and brings the community together.
