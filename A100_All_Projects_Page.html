  • The new A100 Website was a project for and by the Apprentices, who wanted to showcase the idea of training through doing, with a focus on front- and back-end web design. The new webpage was just a start, since many groups have added to the content and functionality of the website since its rollout.

  • The revisitation of the A100 website was one of the ongoing sets of upgrades that this website has received. The content has been improved with such content as a FAQ section and multimedia displays. These additions are still being imporved, and should be added to the current website shortly.

  • This prototype of a new student application system was developed by the Apprentices to try to improve on the current system, hosted by Formstack, and save the associated fees at the same time. While the Apprentices didn't quite manage to beat a commercial product in one month, they did develop several features not found in the current system. A future Cohort will likely revisit this project.

  • The Whiteboard jobs board is another project in the prototype phase, which may be added in the future. The purpose of this was to add a jobs board to the online magazine TheWhiteboardCT.com, which promotes entrepreneurship in Connecticut through stories, relevant information, and the hosting of live events.
