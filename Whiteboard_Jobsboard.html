Team A Few Good Men

Team Members : Jeffrey Poch, Abdel Elleithy, John Devivo

Team a Few Good Men didn't originally start off with the intention of writing a Wordpress plugin, but by the end of the project it just made sense to wrap out code into a plugin so it could be useable in different themes. The plugin basically allows users to create job listings(for employers) and job seeker profiles(for prospective employees). It also allows the Whiteboard to create a section of company profiles - a place where partner companies can have info about them.
