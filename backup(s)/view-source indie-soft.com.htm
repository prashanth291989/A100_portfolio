Our Clients

Intelliblast Health

Matthew Browning, entrepreneur and nurse practitioner, saw a problem in hospital care. In spite of hospitals’ constant need for nurses, there was no communications technology that filled shift vacancies quickly and efficiently. Matt collaborated with Independent Software to create Intelliblast, an innovative web application that revolutionizes hospital-to-nurse communication. His startup, Intelliblast Health, won a patent, secured significant outside investment, and continues to develop its product with Independent Software.


Learn more about our collaboration with Intelliblast Health.

Connecticut’s network of antique barns is a precious, little-known piece of its cultural heritage, which the Connecticut Trust for Historic Preservation wanted to reveal to a wider public. Envisioning an interactive mobile app that would lead users on tours, the Trust needed the technical team to build it. So it turned to Independent Software. The resulting CT Barns iPhone app includes GPS maps linked to curated photo galleries and historical/tourist information, allowing users to discover, both virtually and physically, one of Connecticut’s hidden treasures.


Learn more about our collaboration with the Trust.

Don Jastrebski is an expert in the role culture plays in organizational success. He founded in3 Consulting to help companies build and manage teams using a unique evaluation model called FIT. As the consultancy grew, Don wanted to productize FIT, enhancing its effectiveness and allowing his company to scale up. With the Independent Software team, he created an innovative web application (called inSIGHT) that did just that, transforming in3 from a consultancy to a SaaS provider with a growing clientele.


Learn more about our collaboration with in3 Consulting.

Community-Building

In partnership with the State of Connecticut,
Independent Software runs a range of initiatives to strengthen our startup ecosystem.

A100

Through our A100 Program, we match aspiring software developers with the technical training they need to become "startup-ready." Looking to hire technical talent? A100 is the program for you!

The Whiteboard

Created and run by Independent Software, The Whiteboard is an online community building platform for Connecticut startup ecosystem. By sharing exciting stories of local entrepreneurs, covering news and important events, and providing issue-focused articles, The Whiteboard supports the wins of Connecticut startups and advances entrepreneurship in our State.

2014 Startup Roadshow

The Startup Roadshow is 9-month long initiative of The Whiteboard to celebrate and explore Connecticut's diverse startup communities. Virtually, through The Whiteboard, we bring our readers into a different region of Connecticut each month, profiling top and up-and-coming entrepreneurs and revealing the uniqueness of the place. At the end of each month, we host an event that supports the local entrepreneurs and brings the community together.
